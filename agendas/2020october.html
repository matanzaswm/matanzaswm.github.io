October 1st

Unfortunately, one of our Key Club members has recently passed away. Giovanne Sylvain, a senior of the graduating class of 2021, had a bright future ahead, but his life was unexpectedly cut short. Giovanne joined Key Club in 2017. With a bright personality and innate comfortability to lead and care for those around him, he was truely an admirable person. It is heartbreaking that he will be not be with us during his last year of highschool to see through graduation, and we sincerely hope the best for his family during these bleak times.

We would like to dedicate today as an open meeting for anyone to attend. We will be painting memorial rocks for Giovanne between the times of 2:30pm to 4:00pm. As this is an open meeting, we welcome anyone to come and participate.

Rest in Peace Giovanne. May your smile keep on living!
