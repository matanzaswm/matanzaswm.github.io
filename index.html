About Us

We are the Matanzas Key Clubâ€”the largest student-led service club in Matanzas High School. We provide our members with the opportunities to service, build, and devlop leadership and community skills.

Our core values are based upon our Palm Coast community as well as the initiatives stated by Key Club International. These include: Leadership, Character building, Caring, and Inclusiveness.

Objectives

Key Club International aims to cooperate with school principals and teachers to provide high school students with invaluable experience in living and working together and to prepare them for useful citizenship [1]. These include: giving primacy to human and spirit rather than material values, encourge the golden rule, adoption of higher standards/applications in all social contacts, develop more intelligent and serviceable citizenship, etc.

[1] Courtesey to Key Club International (see for full detail)

What This Means For Us

Matanzas Key Club will uphold these objectives and incorporate them into our own club initiatives. We hope to bring the better of our service to the community and encourge people to take their hands in the matter to experience. We will wish to build teamwork, integrity, and kindness. We will care and bring compassion. We will welcome people of all to join no matter the background. Ultimately, we will be making a positive difference in our own community and to the world.

Club Information

"Why should I join Matanzas Key Club?"

  • Community Service Hours

    Our dedicated members earn approximately 120+ hours of community service throughout highschool! About 1.5 hrs every week if you participate in service projects!

  • Size

    Matanzas Key Club is the largest service club within Matanzas High School with over 50 members and growing!

  • Drives

    Matanzas Key Club has won numerous awards for the most collected food items within the high school division at the Walk for Food.

  • Social

    We are a club that works together, never leaving each other out. Even if you aren't looking for community service, Key Club can be a great place to get to know more people!

  • Integration

    We welcome anybody of any race and gender because we strive to be a club of Inclusiveness. Stop by and get to interact with Matanzas Key Club!

  • Responsibility

    Learn to take charge by gaining leadership and character building skills by getting involved with our board members.
